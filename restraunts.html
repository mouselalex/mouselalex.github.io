Restraunts

The local cuisine of Millard is always changing, however there are some staples that have stood through the test of time. Following is a list of the top 5 restraunts in Millard.

Shirley's Dinner

1. With over 1,206 reviews, and maintaining a 4.5 out of 5 on google reviews Shirley's Dinner is number 1. Serving classic dinner options Shiley's will shirley not disapoint.

Bernies Pizza Parlor

2. Bernies Pizza Parlor is the second generation of the restraunt, forced to close back in 2013 due to raising lease prices, it made a return due to the support of loyal customers.

Finnegan's Pub & Grill

3. Finnegan's has always produced quality food in a fun environment.

The Corner Kick

4. Being the only soccer oriented bar for miles, and serving authentic street styled tacos The Corner Kick is the twist on the classic Mexican Restraunt that many are a fan of.

Smokin Barrel BBQ

5. Hidden off the beaten path, the Smokin Barrel BBQ is a highly apraised BBQ joint that typically only those in the area are known to frequent.
